Tucker Family Charity is a small family-led initiative based in Johannesburg.

ABOUT PAGE

Our Story

Tucker Family Charity began with a simple idea: to support a community project doing important work.

The charity was created by friends and family who wanted to help Oliver’s Village continue its programmes for children and families in the area.

Through fundraising events, donations and merchandise sales, the charity raises funds that go directly towards supporting the centre.

⸻

Our Mission

Our mission is to help provide education, food security and opportunity for children growing up in disadvantaged communities.

By supporting Oliver’s Village, we aim to help children access learning, build skills and grow in a safe environment.

⸻

How the Charity Works

The charity raises funds through:

• community events
• donations from supporters
• merchandise sales
• partnerships with friends and sponsors

Funds raised are directed towards programmes at Oliver’s Village.

⸻

Why It Matters

Education and opportunity change lives.

Many children in the surrounding communities face barriers such as poverty, food insecurity and limited access to resources.

Oliver’s Village helps provide a supportive environment where children can learn, grow and build a better future.

OLIVER’S VILLAGE PAGE

About Oliver’s Village

Oliver’s Village is a community education centre located near Benoni, east of Johannesburg.

The organisation works with children and families from nearby communities to provide education, training and practical support.

The project began as Oliver’s House in 2001 and has grown into a broader community centre supporting hundreds of people.

⸻

What Oliver’s Village Provides

Early Childhood Development

The centre provides early education for young children, helping them develop the skills needed for school.

Computer Training

Computer facilities provide digital skills training for young people and adults.

Food Support

A feeding programme ensures children attending the centre receive regular meals.

Community Support

The centre also supports families through community programmes and skills training.

⸻

The Impact

Oliver’s Village supports many members of the surrounding community every day.

Children receive education and meals.
Young people gain digital skills.
Families receive practical support.

These programmes help build stronger futures for the community.
